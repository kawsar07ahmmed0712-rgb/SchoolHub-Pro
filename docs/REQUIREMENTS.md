# Requirements (SchoolHub)

## School Structure
- Total classes: 10
- Total sections: 20
- Students per section: 50
- Each class has exactly 1 Class Teacher (owner)

## Daily Routine & Accountability
- 6 periods per day
- Subject teachers take periods based on timetable
- Each teacher submits a daily teaching log (periods + topic + checkbox submit)
- Headmaster reviews teaching logs and checks teacher attendance
- Teachers record student attendance per section per day
